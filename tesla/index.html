Nikola Tesla

Nikola Tesla was a Serbian-American inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, and futurist who is best known for his contributions to the design of the modern alternating current electricity supply system.

Here are some of the most incredible creations of Nikola Tesla:

  • Alternating Current (1883)
  • Teleautomaton (1898)
  • The Induction Motor (1888)
  • The Adams Power Plant Transformer House (1896)
  • Neon Lamps (1893)
  • Radio (1895)
  • Shadowgraphs (1895)
  • The Tesla Turbine (1909)
  • The Magnifying Transmitter
  • The Tesla Coil (1891)
